About

Hello, is it me you're looking for?

My name is Cristina Ponay and I hail from the Philippines. I have a bachelor's degree in IT and have over 10 years of software development experience. Eight years of which were spent in Singapore, which I considered my second home. In 2018, I decided to change my life, left Singapore, and moved to Canada to study. I'm currently based in Halifax, NS and will finish the IT Data Analytics program at Nova Scotia Community College in May 2020.

I have always enjoyed programming. I like how it forces me think of solutions to problems. In my spare time, I take online courses to keep up with the latest programming technologies and also to keep my programming skills sharp. My favorite programming languages at the moment are Python, JavaScript, and SQL.

Other than programming, I love traveling, photography, hiking, exercise, cooking, and eating.
